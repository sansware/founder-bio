## Why this course matters to my work

GSD1x sits behind the "architectural training applied to software systems" framing on the live `/jordan` page. The course teaches architectural imagination as a discipline — reading buildings as arguments, treating spatial decisions as systems of constraints, and learning to design at the level of the relationships between elements rather than the elements themselves.

That mode of thinking transfers directly to how I architect web platforms: components, constraints, and the relationships between them, before any aesthetic decision. The credential is here primarily as evidence; the methodology it shaped is described in the live bio and in the frameworks I have since published.
